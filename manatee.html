The McQueen Manatee Page

In June of 1997 the McQueen Clan spent time in Florida. This bit of video was shot at the state park in Homosassa Springs, Florida.

Quicktime movie of a manatee (1.4 Meg)

This movie does play under Apple's QuickTime program. I've tried to play it on Microsoft's Internet Explorer V 4.0, and the sound didn't play. If you have this problem, right-click on the ActiveMovie Control (the movie player), select "Save As" to save the movie to your hard disk, then play it on the real thing from Apple.
